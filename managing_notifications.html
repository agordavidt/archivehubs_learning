Managing Notifications and Activity Logs

Learn how to effectively manage your notifications and track your activity logs to stay organized and informed on Archivehubs.

Notification Management

Archivehubs provides comprehensive notification management tools that help you stay informed about important activities while avoiding information overload. You can customize notification preferences to match your workflow and priorities.

Notification Settings

Customize which notifications you receive and how they are delivered

Smart Filtering

Automatically filter notifications based on importance and relevance

Timing Controls

Set quiet hours and notification schedules to avoid interruptions

Activity Logs and Tracking

Keep track of your digital asset activities, interactions, and platform usage through comprehensive activity logs. This helps you monitor your productivity and maintain a clear record of your professional activities.

  • View detailed activity history with timestamps and descriptions
  • Track file uploads, downloads, and sharing activities
  • Monitor engagement with your shared content
  • Export activity reports for professional documentation
  • Set up activity alerts for important milestones
