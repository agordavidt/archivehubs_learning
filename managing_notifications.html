Managing Notifications and Activity Logs

Managing Notifications & Activity Logs on Archivehubs.com

Video Placeholder

Efficient notification and activity log management ensure users stay informed and in control of their digital interactions on Archivehubs.com.

  • Click the bell icon on the dashboard to view recent alerts.
  • Notifications include file shares, comments, purchases, collaboration requests, and monetization updates.
  • Customize preferences to receive alerts via email, push notifications, or in-app messages.
  • Navigate to the Activity Log section to track all recent actions.
  • View a detailed history of uploads, downloads, shares, purchases, and edits.
  • Filter logs by date, activity type, or user interactions.
  • Adjust settings in the User Preferences panel.
  • Enable or disable specific alerts to minimize distractions.
  • Set priority notifications for important activities like sales, security alerts, and collaboration updates.

By efficiently managing notifications and activity logs, users can stay organized, enhance collaboration, and optimize productivity on Archivehubs.com.
