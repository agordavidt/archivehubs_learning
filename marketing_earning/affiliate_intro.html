Affiliate Marketing & Earning Opportunities Introduction

Welcome to the Archivehubs.com Affiliate Marketing Program — a powerful opportunity for digital marketers, influencers, and content creators to earn commissions by referring creators, businesses, and digital professionals to our marketplace. Archivehubs.com is a trusted platform for selling, licensing, and monetizing digital assets, and our affiliate program is designed to reward partners who help expand our creator community.

As an affiliate partner, you will promote Archivehubs.com to private sector audiences — including freelancers, creative agencies, startups, digital entrepreneurs, and media professionals. Please note that our affiliate program is not intended for use with government institutions. Partners are expected to focus exclusively on private and commercial sectors for all outreach, referrals, and promotional efforts.

Whether you're driving traffic through content, referrals, or digital campaigns, our multi-tiered commission system, real-time tracking tools, and milestone-based rewards offer a scalable path to passive income and long-term success.

How to Become a Licensed Archivehubs Certified Affiliate Marketer

Affiliate Marketing Network

To earn the title of Archivehubs Certified Affiliate Marketer, candidates must complete a structured learning and evaluation process designed to ensure deep understanding of the platform and effective promotional strategies as express below.

Certification Requirements

Candidates must successfully complete all four foundational Labs:

  • Lab 1: Archivehubs Fundamentals: Understand the platform, its mission, and the core features that drive digital asset monetization.
  • Lab 2: Content and Portfolio Management: Learn how to manage, optimize, and present digital content effectively on Archivehubs.com.
  • Lab 3: Archivehubs Optimization: Gain skills in maximizing asset visibility, improving searchability, and driving sales performance.
  • Lab 4: Affiliate Marketing and Opportunities: Explore affiliate marketing techniques, commission structures, and ethical promotion strategies (focused solely on the private sector, not government institutions).

After completing the labs, candidates must take an online examination that assesses knowledge from all four areas.

  • A minimum score of 60% is required to pass.
  • Candidates are given 3 attempts to pass the exam.
  • If a candidate fails all 3 attempts, they must wait one full month before reapplying for the next round of testing.

Upon passing the exam:

  • Candidates will be awarded the official Archivehubs Certified Affiliate Marketer Certificate with an Affiliate ID.
  • They will gain access to advanced affiliate dashboards, referral codes and Links, promotional materials, and bonus commission tiers.
  • Certified affiliates are added to the official partner list and may be invited to exclusive campaigns or events or awards.

This structured process ensures that certified affiliate marketers are well-equipped, credible, and aligned with Archivehubs' brand, standards and policies.
