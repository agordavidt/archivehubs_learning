    Getting Started with Archivehubs

    Interactive Tutorial: Getting Started with Archivehubs.com

    Video Placeholder

    This tutorial will guide new users through the key steps to navigate and utilize Archivehubs.com effectively.

    • Visit Archivehubs.com and click Sign Up.
    • Enter your details and verify your email.
    • Set up your profile and preferences
    • Get a walkthrough of the dashboard layout and key features.
    • Customize your settings and notifications for a personalized experience.
    • Click "Upload" to add digital assets securely.
    • Use AI-powered tagging to categorize and search files easily.
    • View updates on shared files, collaborations, and industry news in your feed.
    • Manage notifications to stay informed about important activities.
    • Connect with professionals and join industry communities.
    • Share assets, set permissions, and engage with team members.
    • Learn how to sell or license your digital content.
    • Track earnings and manage transactions effortlessly.
    • Use the Help Center for FAQs and troubleshooting.
    • Join live support sessions or chat with customer service.
